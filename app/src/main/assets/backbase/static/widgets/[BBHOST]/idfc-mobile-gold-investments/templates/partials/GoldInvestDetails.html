Declaration


I/We hereby declare and undertake that

  1. my/our aggregate investment in Sovereign Gold bonds does not exceed 4000 Grams during the current financial year

  2. the Information furnish in this application form is correct

  3. I have read and understood the features of the scheme, the details of information for the investors as well as rights and duties of investors.

Terms and Conditions


  1. The Sovereign Gold Bond (SGB) product is a third party product offered by RBI and IDFC Bank is not liable or responsible and shall not be held responsible or liable for the product.

  2. Only application received within the range of, 1 grams and 4000 grams, shall be accepted.

    3. In case of insufficient funds in the account, the request will not be processed.

  3. Only Single holding mode is allowed for online mode of the SGB product. The subscription to SGB shall be made in the name of the account holder who is applying for SGB under his/her login.

  4. The nomination details for the corresponding account from which funds are debited (funding account) will automatically be applicable, provided that the nominee details are updated in IDFC Bank's system. Other details required by RBI for the purpose of issuance of SGB (e.g. Bank account number, address, contact details, PAN no etc.) shall be provided as per the details captured under the funding account.

  5. The account number from where the funds are debited for subscribing for sovereign gold bond, those account number will be taken for receiving the interest pertaining to SGB also Maturity redemption will also happen in the same account.

  6. If the application is rejected from RBI on account of breach for the maximum investment unit in the respective financial year permissible clause, then IDFC bank will not be held liable.

Rights of the Investors


  1. Holding Certificate will be issued to the investor by Reserve Bank of India.

  2. The interest on the bond is paid half-yearly. The servicing of the Interest will be done through the bank A/c provided in the Application for or in through the A/c linked to the demat, as the case may be.

  3. Application forms for investments under Sovereign Gold Bonds are available on the website at https://myidfcbank.com

  4. A sole holder or all the joint holders may nominate maximum of two nominees to the rights of the bonds.

  5. The nomination will be registered at the Office of Issue and a Certificate of Registration will be issued to the holder.

  6. The nomination can be altered by registering a fresh nomination.

  7. The existing nomination can be cancelled by a request to the Office of Issue.

Information for Investors of Sovereign Gold Bonds 2016


  1. Category of Investor: The Bonds will be restricted for sale to resident Indian entities including individuals, HUFs, trusts, charitable institutions and Universities.

  2. Limit of investment: Minimum subscription 1 Grams and Maximum investment 4000 Grams per investor per annum.

  3. Date of Issue of bonds: Date of issue of bond will be the same which is inscribed on the holding certificate.

  4. Interest Option: Half yearly Interval, will be served directly in to the account mentioned in the application form or in the Account linked with the Demat a/c.

  5. Post Maturity Interest: Post Maturity Interest is not payable.

  6. Bank account: It is mandatory for the investors to provide bank account details to facilitate payment of interest /maturity value.

  7. Nomination Facility: The sole Holder or all the joint holders may nominate a maximum of two persons as nominee.

  8. Maturity period: 8 years from date of issue.

  9. Premature redemption: Allowed on the coupon dates from the 5th year of issuance.

  10. Tradability: Trading of these bonds on exchanges/NDS-OM shall be notified.

  11. Loans from banks against the security of these bonds: The holders of the said securities shall be entitled to create pledge, hypothecation or lien in favour of scheduled banks.
